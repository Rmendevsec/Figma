## Typography 
- The art and science of arranging text to be both legible and appeling
- Style or appearance of text
- The art of working with text

## Common Types of Fonts

- **Serif**: have little stroke called serifs attached to the main part of the letter
    - It is good for traditional projects. They're also common in print publications like magazines and newspapers
- **San Serif**: don't have that extra stroke - hence the name, which is French for "without serif". This considerd ad more clean and modern than serif. Also easy to read in all devices

- **Display Fonts**:mcome in many different styles like script, blackletter, all-caps and just plain fancy. The're best for small amount of text. for example titles and headers and more graphic-heavy designs.
